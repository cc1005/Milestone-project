About us

The Irish Newspaper Forum is an academic organisation dedicated to furthering research into the hitherto neglected field of Irish journalism. We run an annual conference in October/November, and accept both contemporary and historical papers to be given on topics ranging from Irish local history to international journalism with a slight Irish aspect. We also organise frequent events around Ireland and the United Kingdom, and have a wide range of publications arising out of both previous conferences and the efforts of individual forum members.
